Business-in-a-Box — Full Feasibility & Revenue Dashboard

Based on your current services: Software & App Development · SMB India Market · Branding · Web · SEO · Marketing

What is Business-in-a-Box?

A productised service model where Baptist Digitek bundles its existing capabilities — website, app, branding, SEO, ads, and automation — into a single, industry-specific package sold to small and medium businesses across India. Instead of quoting custom projects every time, you sell a ready-made "digital kit" that gets a business live and operational within weeks.

How does the revenue work?

There are two income streams: a one-time setup fee (₹40k–₹1.5L per kit) paid when you onboard a new client, and a monthly retainer (₹15k–₹30k/month) for ongoing support, ads, and maintenance. Over time, retainer income stacks up and becomes the primary, predictable revenue base — reaching a ₹1 Crore annual run rate by Month 10.

Complete workflow — from zero to operating

Step-by-step operational process for Baptist Digitek to launch and run Business-in-a-Box.

1
Choose your first niche
Do not launch all kits at once. Pick the one closest to your existing client portfolio. Most likely: healthcare clinics or cafés.
  • Audit existing clients — what industry has the most?
  • Choose: Clinic, Café, Retail, or Creator
  • Interview 3 potential clients in that niche — confirm pain points
Week 1 No cost
2
Build your first kit template
One-time investment. Build the reusable structure that makes delivery fast and profitable from the second client onward.
  • Brand kit: 3–5 base brand concepts for the industry (colour systems, logo structure)
  • Website template: Industry wireframe with all pages pre-structured (just fill in content)
  • SEO template: Pre-written keyword research + page structure for the industry
  • Social kit: 30-day content calendar template + Canva / Figma post templates
  • Ad templates: 3 Meta campaign structures + copy templates for that industry
  • WhatsApp flow: Pre-built message flow for appointment / order reminders
  • Onboarding deck: 10-slide client onboarding presentation, questionnaire, timeline
Weeks 2–4 Internal effort — 2–3 team members
3
Define pricing & packages
Package into 3 tiers. Never quote custom every time — this is what makes the model scalable.
  • Starter Kit: Branding + Website + Google Business — ₹40–75k one-time, ₹8–15k/mo
  • Growth Kit: Starter + App + SEO + Social + Ads — ₹75–1.5L one-time, ₹20–40k/mo
  • Scale Kit: Full ecosystem + automation + monthly campaigns — ₹1.5–3L one-time, ₹40–80k/mo
  • Write a 1-page pricing sheet per industry kit
  • Set up CRM: Zoho or HubSpot Free to track leads and retainer billing
Week 3 Defines your revenue ceiling
4
Land your first 3 BiB clients
Before spending on ads, use warm outreach. First 3 clients validate the model, create your case studies, and fund future growth.
  • Message existing contacts — "We have a new complete business package for clinics/cafés"
  • Offer a 20% launch discount for the first 3 clients (document everything as case studies)
  • Post on LinkedIn: "We're launching a 14-day business setup system for [niche]"
  • List on Justdial, IndiaMart, Clutch — as a productized service, not just "web dev"
  • Join 2 local business WhatsApp groups in your target city and share the offer
Month 1–2 First revenue
5
Deliver the first kit (controlled)
First delivery is about perfecting the process — speed and quality. Document every step for the playbook.
  • Week 1: Client discovery call + onboarding questionnaire + brand options
  • Week 2: Brand approved → website build begins → social profiles created
  • Week 3: Website live → WhatsApp bot deployed → SEO setup done → ads templates handed over
  • Week 4: Review + launch + handover → transition to monthly retainer
  • Record every bottleneck — improve template for the next client
4 weeks per client Builds the playbook
6
Convert setup clients to retainers
Retainers = recurring, predictable revenue. Every setup client must be migrated to a monthly retainer at delivery.
  • Present retainer options during launch week (not after) — when client satisfaction is highest
  • Retainer includes: SEO maintenance, monthly content (8 posts), ad optimisation, performance report
  • Offer 3-month retainer upfront at a slight discount to lock in cash flow
  • Set up automated monthly invoice + report via Zoho / Razorpay Subscriptions
Month 2 onward Recurring income begins
7
Build case studies & launch marketing
After 3 successful kits, you have proof. Now market aggressively using real results.
  • Publish 1 case study per kit on your website — before/after, timeline, results, client quote
  • Launch a dedicated "Business-in-a-Box" landing page with industry selector
  • Run Google Ads: "complete digital setup for clinics India" — ₹20–40k/month budget
  • Start LinkedIn content: "We launched a clinic in 14 days — here's how"
  • Referral programme: existing clients refer → 1 month free retainer for them
Month 3–4 Growth begins here
8
Launch second industry kit & scale
By Month 4–5, the first kit runs on autopilot. Now build Kit #2. Repeat the process. Each new kit multiplies addressable market.
  • Apply same template system to second niche (e.g. Café after Clinic)
  • Hire 1 dedicated account manager to manage retainer clients
  • Scale ads budget as retainer income covers it
  • Explore partnerships with chartered accountants, business consultants who refer SMB clients
Month 5+ Revenue compounds

Why retainers matter

Every setup client you convert to a retainer adds permanent monthly income. 20 retainer clients at ₹20k/month = ₹4 lakh/month guaranteed — before you sign a single new setup project. That's financial stability.

Add-on revenue

Once a client is in your ecosystem, upselling is natural. Apps, video shoots, AI chatbots, influencer campaigns — each adds ₹25k–2L on top of the retainer. Target 30% of clients buying 1 add-on per year.

Referral multiplier

SMB owners talk to each other — CA networks, trade associations, local business groups. One happy clinic owner in Chennai refers 3 others. Build a formal referral programme from Month 3 onward.
